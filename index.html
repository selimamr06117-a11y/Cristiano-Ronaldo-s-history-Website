Who is Cristiano Ronaldo ?

Cristiano Ronaldo is a famous Portuguese football player. His full name is Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro . He was born in Funchal, Madeira Island, Portugal. In year 1985

Where Cristiano Ronaldo played ?

Cristiano Ronaldo started his football career at Sporting Lisbon, then moved to Manchester United and performed brilliantly, which made Real Madrid buy him. He won 4 Ballon d'Ors with them, then some problems occurred with the management, so he moved to Juventus, then returned to his former club, Manchester United, then moved and still plays until now with Al-Nasr Saudi Club, What many people do not know is that he played in the Andorra Academy in Portugal when he was a child.

Cristiano Ronaldo's nicknames :

Cristiano Ronaldo is nicknamed "El Don" in Portugal and around the world, an honorary title meaning "master" or "leader" and given with respect to distinguished figures. He is also known as "The Rocket of Madeira" due to his speed, strength, and his hometown of Madeira. Other common nicknames: CR7: An abbreviation of the first two letters of his name and his famous jersey number (7). "Crybaby": A nickname given to him by his teammates on his childhood team, Andorinha, because he cried a lot when the team didn't win.
